What are the Commonwealth Games?
A grand sports festival of Commonwealth of Nations member countries and the British Empire countries.
Who first proposed Commonwealth Games? (1891)
Reverend Astley Cooper published the idea in "The Times" magazine.
First implementation attempt? (1911)
During coronation celebrations of King George V in London.
Who organized first Games? (1928)
Canada's Melville Marcus Robinson - named "British Empire Games".
First Games location & year?
1930 - Hamilton, Canada (called "British Olympic Games")
Stadium: Civic Stadium | Events: 59 competitions
Participating countries in 1930?
11 countries | 27 sports (Rowing & Diving held separately)
