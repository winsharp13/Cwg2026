Who first came up with the idea of organizing the Common Health Games? This idea came to Father Reverend Astley Cooper in 1891. The idea was published in a magazine called "The Times".

He tried to implement this idea during the coronation celebrations of "Samat Pancham Jayoj" held in London in 1911.

In 1928, Canada's Melville Marcus Robinson made a special request to hold the Commonwealth Games under the name "British Empire Games".

The first Commonwealth Games were held in 1930, "British Olympic Games" in Hamilton, Canada. The Cup is held in Kupare. Commonwealth Games Federation.
